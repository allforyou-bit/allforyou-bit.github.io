About the 308A Red Seal Exam

The 308A Refrigeration and Air Conditioning Mechanic Red Seal exam covers refrigeration cycles, refrigerants, compressors, controls, and system troubleshooting. The exam is 120 multiple-choice questions requiring a 70% pass mark. Knowledge of environmental regulations around refrigerants is also tested.
