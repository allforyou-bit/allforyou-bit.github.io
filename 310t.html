About the 310T Red Seal Exam

The 310T Truck and Transport Mechanic Red Seal exam covers service and repair of heavy trucks, trailers, and transport equipment. The exam is 130 multiple-choice questions and requires a minimum 70% to pass. Key topics include air brake systems, diesel engines, electrical systems, and DOT compliance regulations.
