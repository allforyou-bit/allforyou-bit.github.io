About the 421A Red Seal Exam

The Red Seal Heavy Equipment Technician (421A) exam is Canada's nationally recognized certification for diagnosing, repairing, and maintaining heavy duty equipment. The exam consists of 135 multiple-choice questions based on the 2023 Red Seal Occupational Standard (RSOS). Use this free practice tool to test your knowledge by topic and difficulty level.

🔧 Concept Review — Torque Converter

A torque converter is a fluid coupling between the engine and automatic transmission. It has three main components:

  • Pump (Impeller) — Connected to the engine. Spins fluid outward.
  • Turbine — Connected to the transmission input shaft. Driven by fluid.
  • Stator — Sits between pump and turbine. Redirects fluid to multiply torque.
Stall Test ResultMeaning
RPM lower than specEngine or converter lacks power/torque (engine problem)
RPM higher than specStator clutch is slipping — no torque multiplication
RPM normalSystem is healthy
Coupling Phase: When turbine speed gets close to pump speed (~90%), the stator begins to rotate freely (overrun clutch releases). Torque multiplication stops — converter acts as a simple fluid coupling.
