About Canada Red Seal Exam Prep

A free, apprentice-built study resource for every Canadian skilled trades worker preparing for Red Seal certification.

Who We Are

🔧
Site Creator

Working 421A Heavy Equipment Technician Apprentice

This site was built by a working Heavy Equipment Technician (421A) apprentice currently in the intermediate stages of the apprenticeship program. With hands-on experience servicing construction equipment, hydraulic systems, diesel engines, and electrical systems, the content here reflects what's actually tested — not just textbook theory.

Experience areas: Excavators, motor graders, crawler dozers, articulated haulers — diesel engine diagnostics, hydraulic system troubleshooting, electrical fault finding, and preventive maintenance scheduling.

The idea for this site came out of frustration: when preparing for the Red Seal 421A exam, it was nearly impossible to find free, comprehensive practice questions that actually matched the exam format and the 2023 Red Seal Occupational Standard (RSOS). Most resources were either paywalled, outdated, or too generic to be useful.

So I built this one myself — and decided to make it free for every apprentice in Canada.

What We Cover

Canada Red Seal Exam Prep currently provides free practice questions and career guides for the following Red Seal trades:

  • 421A — Heavy Equipment Technician (220 questions): Diesel engines, hydraulics, electrical, powertrain, brakes, safety
  • 310T — Truck & Transport Mechanic (165 questions): Air brakes, diesel engines, drivetrain, DOT compliance, electrical
  • 309A — Construction Electrician (135 questions): Canadian Electrical Code, motors, wiring methods, safety theory
  • 310S — Automotive Service Technician (135 questions): Engine, brakes, electrical, suspension, transmission
  • 308A — Refrigeration & AC Mechanic (115 questions): Refrigerants, compressors, controls, troubleshooting

There's also an Exam Registration Guide covering provincial contacts, what to bring, fees, and what happens after you pass. Plus in-depth career guides for each trade covering apprenticeship requirements, wage data, and exam strategies.

How We Create Our Questions

All practice questions on this site are created using the following sources and methodology:

  1. 2023 Red Seal Occupational Standards (RSOS) — the official national standard that defines what each trade must know
  2. Provincial apprenticeship curriculum — technical training materials from multiple provinces
  3. Real-world shop experience — questions reflect practical scenarios encountered in actual field work
  4. Community feedback — questions are updated based on suggestions from apprentices who have recently written the exam
Important: These practice questions are designed for study purposes and are not official exam questions. I aim for accuracy but recommend cross-referencing with official RSOS documents and your provincial apprenticeship authority.
