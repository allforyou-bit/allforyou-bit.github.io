About This Site

421A Red Seal Exam Prep is a free study resource for Canadian Heavy Equipment Technicians preparing for the Red Seal 421A certification exam.

What We Offer

Our platform provides free practice questions covering all major topics of the Red Seal 421A Heavy Equipment Technician exam, based on the 2023 Red Seal Occupational Standard (RSOS):

  • Diesel Engine Systems
  • Hydraulic Systems
  • Electrical & Electronic Systems
  • Powertrain & Torque Converters
  • Brakes, Steering & Suspension
  • Safety & Common Occupational Skills
  • Preventive Maintenance

Our Mission

We believe every technician deserves access to high-quality, free exam preparation resources. The Red Seal 421A certification opens doors to better career opportunities across Canada, and we're here to help you pass on your first attempt.

About the 421A Red Seal Exam

The Red Seal Heavy Equipment Technician (421A) is Canada's nationally recognized certification for diagnosing, repairing, and maintaining heavy duty equipment. The exam consists of 135 multiple-choice questions and is administered across all provinces and territories.

For official exam information, visit red-seal.ca.
