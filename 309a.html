About the 309A Red Seal Exam

The 309A Construction Electrician Red Seal exam tests knowledge of the Canadian Electrical Code (CEC), electrical theory, wiring methods, motor controls, and safety. The exam is 120 multiple-choice questions with a 70% passing score. It is one of the most written Red Seal exams in Canada.
